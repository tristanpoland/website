    Starting the Genesis Roadmap Calls

    Aug 20 2019

    image credit oxana v (@arttravelling), via Unsplash

    This month (August of 2019), we started holding regularly scheduled Roadmap Calls, where the developers and release engineers working on Genesis provide updates, explain where we are at in the roadmap, where we want to go, and answer questions form anyone who pops in.

    We'll be holding these calls on the 3rd Tuesday of every month at 11am EDT. More details can be found on the Community Roadmap Call page (on genesisproject.io).

    We hope that these calls help to put faces to names, and remind everyone involves that the people who create Genesis and the people who use Genesis are just that: people. It's all too easy to get lost in the clean lines of GitHub and forget that behind those screen names are living, breathing human beings.

    We also want to have a forum in which to discuss the direction of the project, its velocity in pursuing that direction, and more. So stop on in, say hi, and join in the fun!

    Genesis is maintained by Stark & Wayne, a leading Cloud-Native consulting firm.
